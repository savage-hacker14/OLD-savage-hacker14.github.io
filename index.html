About me

I am a senior at Glastonbury High School, Class of 2020. Since an early age, I have had an interest for technology. As a toddler, I was already using a computer. Then in 4th grade I was introduced to Scratch, a graphical programming language. Since then, my affinity for programming increased and for my 14th birthday I received a Raspberry Pi. I truly learned a lot from this credit-card sized computer and it has really shaped who I am! Today I develop software applications for US FIRST Robotics and personal projects.
